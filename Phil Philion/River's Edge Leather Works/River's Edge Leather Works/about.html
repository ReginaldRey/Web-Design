Our Background

Who we are & What we do

Having worked in the trades my whole life, I was constantly searching for clothing and accessories that could stand the test of time. Not satisfied with what was available, I decided to make my own. Over time this hobby grew, as friends and family asked me to make them their own attire, and eventually I decided to branch out and found River's Edge. Now, with the help of my beautiful wife, we're poised to bring our love of leather working to everyone. Nothing makes us happier than hearing from our satisfied clients. If you've ordered from us recently, reach out and let us know how we did!
